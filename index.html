Tracking

Tips

For best results, use a reticle eyepiece and precision align the EQ platfrom before tuning the tracking rate. Prevent any external movement of the scope while it is tracking during the tuning procedure - consider using clutches if you have them or tune on a night with very little wind.

Instructions

Set up the scope and EQ platform as normal. Set tracking to "stellar." Choose a star near the celestial equator and center it in the eyepiece. Click the "set start" button. Allow the platform to track for some time. Once a star is no longer centered, use only the arrow keys on the tracking menu to re-center the star as best you can. Do not push the scope by hand. If the platform is not perfectly aligned, you may be unable to re-center the star. This is OK; it will not affect tuning accuracy as long as you position the star as close to center as you can using the arrow keys only. Once the star is near-centered, press the "set end" button. The platform will then calculate and save the new tracking rate.

More Tips

The platform will not calculate and save a new tracking rate until "set end" is pressed. When "set end" is pressed, it will use the most recent "set start" and the time elapsed between to calculate a new rate. In other words, the "set start" and arrow buttons will have no effect on tracking rate until "set end" is pressed.
